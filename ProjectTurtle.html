There are 356 species of turtles in the world.
The 7 sea turtles species are; green, hawksbill, kemp's ridley, leatherback, loggerhead, olive ridley and the flatback.

Five major threats to sea turtles are;
fisheries bycatch, coastal development, pollution & pathogens, direct take and climate change.
